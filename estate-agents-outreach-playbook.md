## 📧 Cold Email Variants

### Variant 1: Valuation Focus (Primary)
**Use for:** Most targets, especially those with visible valuation tools

```
Subject: quick question

Hi {{first_name}},

Quick one — are you currently doing anything proactive to generate more valuation appointments, or mainly relying on Rightmove, Zoopla, and referrals?

We help estate agents create a steadier flow of seller and landlord conversations through targeted outbound.

Worth a quick chat?

– {{your_name}}
```

**Why it works:**
- Short (under 50 words)
- Question-based (invites response)
- Names their pain (portal dependency)
- Low pressure ("worth a chat?")

Just bumping this in case it got buried.

Would it be useful to see how we're helping agencies create more valuation and seller conversations?

```

### Follow-Up 2 (Day 7-8)
```
Subject: Re: {{original_subject}}

No worries if this isn't a priority right now.

- Respectful, not pushy

## ⚠️ Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ DO:
- Keep emails under 75 words
- Ask questions (invite response)
- Personalize based on real observations
- Follow up 2-3 times
- Track everything in the CSV

### ❌ DON'T:
- Send generic "I noticed your website" without specifics
- Use spammy language ("guaranteed", "secret", "explode")
- Attach files or images (triggers spam filters)
- Send more than 50 emails/day from one account
- Forget to track replies and outcomes

## 📞 Call Script (When They Reply)

**When they say "Yes, tell me more":**

```
"Thanks for getting back. Quick context:

We help estate agents who are tired of relying solely on portals and referrals.

We build outbound systems that:
- Find homeowners/landlords before they list
- Reach out with personalized messaging
- Book valuation appointments directly on your calendar

Most of our clients add 10-20 extra valuation conversations per month.

What's your current situation with valuations — mostly portal-driven, or doing anything proactive?"
```

**Qualifying questions:**
1. How many valuations do you do per month currently?
2. What % come from portals vs referrals vs other?
3. What would 10-20 more valuations/month mean for your business?
4. Have you tried anything outbound before?

**Close:**
```
"Sounds like this could help. Want to do a 15-min call next week to see if it's a fit?

I can show you exactly how we'd approach it for {{company}}."
```
